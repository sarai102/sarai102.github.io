Cook Counseling at Virginia Tech

45 mins

Cook Counseling Center has many resources and outside resources that can help get a student through their college experience while taking care of their mental, physical, and intellectual health. My favorite resource are the workshops they have throughout semester. One that is very importnat is the skills building such as cooking or dealing with anxiety and time management around exam time. Feel Free to make an appointment or browse the site just to familiarize yourself with the free resources.
